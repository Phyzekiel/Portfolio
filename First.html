Welcome!

I am currently a Mechanical Specialist at S&B Engineers and Constructors.

Becoming a Mechanical Engineer


My Master's Degree allowed me to transition from being a career physics educator to a machine design engineer. It was quite a change in scenery!

I have a passion for learning, so I approach new challenges as a fun and engaging opportunity! In grade school, I taught myself HTML and Excel in college, and taught myself 3D modeling and 3D printing during my Master's Degree too!

I ♥ Physics!



I've always loved Physics, from momentum up to string theory! If anything, I have a tendency to over-analyze and collect too much information when tackling a problem. Years of Physics and Biomechanics studies and teaching has burned that habit into my firmware.
