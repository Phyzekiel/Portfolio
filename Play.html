Martial Artist


I love the martial arts. I am a 4th Dan black belt in Taekwon do, 2nd degree black belt in Hapki do, and I did Kendo and Judo for one year each.

I have never stopped practicing for the past 22 years, and hope I never will. I love the feeling I get after practicing my empty hand forms and sword forms.

My favorite proverb from martial arts: Be healthy like the river. The flowing water never gets very dirty, because it never stops moving. Be like the river. Keep moving, and stay healthy

Parkour - The Art of Movement


I started doing parkour with my friends almost 20 years ago, as a branch off of learning tumbling and extreme martial arts moves.

I love flipping too, but after meeting David Belle in Lisses, France and training there, I became a Parkour purist. He said, Et forte pour etre utilé. (Be strong, to be useful)

I prefer to move with purpose, quickly and smoothly, no matter what obstacles I face. It’s a great metaphor for how to live a fulfilling successful life.
